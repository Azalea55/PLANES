AIRBUS

AIRBUS A320:The Airbus A320 is one of the most successful and widely used commercial jetliners in aviation history. It's a narrow-body, twin-engine jet designed for short- to medium-haul routes and is the backbone of many airline fleets around the world.

AIRBUS A321:The Airbus A321 is the largest member of the A320 family and has become a major player in both short- and long-haul markets, especially in its newer A321neo and A321XLR forms. It's widely regarded as one of the most versatile and fuel-efficient narrow-body aircraft available.
